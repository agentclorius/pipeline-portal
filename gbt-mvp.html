📋 Tickets Module

Ticket Types

  • IN SCOPE
    Box Returns
    Single employee returns — employee receives box, returns device
  • IN SCOPE
    Bulk Returns
    Office clear-outs, multiple devices from one location
  • IN SCOPE
    Relocation
    Move devices between locations or to deploy partner
    📌 GBT needs monthly export to their deploy partner
  • DEFERRED
    Redeployment
    Send refurbished device to new employee
    May come later — not required for initial launch

Ticket Creation Methods

  • IN SCOPE
    Bulk CSV Upload
    Upload list of tickets via CSV/Excel file
  • IN SCOPE
    AI-Powered Upload
    Upload messy files, AI maps columns automatically
  • IN SCOPE
    API / Integration
    Tickets created automatically from GBT systems (FreshService)
  • NOT NEEDED
    Manual Entry
    Single ticket creation via form — not required for GBT

🏭 Warehouse Module (Return Hub ERP)

Full warehouse functionality required for Scandic IT to process GBT devices.

Logistics

  • IN SCOPE
    Outbound Labels
    Generate and send shipping labels to employees
  • IN SCOPE
    Inbound Tracking
    Track when boxes arrive at warehouse
  • IN SCOPE
    Pickup Booking
    Employee books courier pickup via link in email
  • IN SCOPE
    Drop-off / Parcel Shop
    Employee finds nearest drop-off point via link

Processing

  • IN SCOPE
    Intake / Receiving
    Register what arrives at warehouse
  • IN SCOPE
    Data Erasure
    Certified data destruction with certificates
  • IN SCOPE
    Diagnostics
    Test devices, capture hardware health
  • IN SCOPE
    Grading
    Grade devices A/B/C/D based on condition
  • IN SCOPE
    Repair Tracking
    Track repairs performed on devices

Inventory

  • IN SCOPE
    Stock Management
    Track inventory levels and locations
  • IN SCOPE
    Monthly Export to Deploy Partner
    Export supported devices for transfer to GBT's deployment partner
    📌 Critical workflow — devices leave Returna inventory monthly
