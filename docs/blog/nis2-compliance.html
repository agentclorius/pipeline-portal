NIS2 & IT Asset Disposition

How Returna helps you meet EU cybersecurity requirements for retired devices

The EU Network and Information Security Directive 2 (NIS2) requires organizations to implement comprehensive cybersecurity measures — including supply chain security and risk management for all IT systems.

But what happens when devices leave your organization?

Every retired laptop, returned employee device, or decommissioned server is a potential compliance gap:

✕ Data breaches from improperly wiped devices
✕ Supply chain vulnerabilities through unvetted disposal partners
✕ Audit failures due to missing documentation
✕ Regulatory penalties for non-compliance
€10M or 2% of global turnover
Maximum penalty for NIS2 non-compliance
